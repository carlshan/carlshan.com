About

My name is Carl. I'm currently a 2014 Fellow at the University of Chicago's Data Science for Social Good program, working under Rayid Ghani at the Center for Data Science and Public Policy. I am developing my statistics & computer science skills so that I can contribute to societal issues around poverty, health and education.

You can read the first in a series of posts chronicling my experiences as a DSSG Fellow here. You can also subscribe to get new writings here!

In the past, I've worked at:

  • Clever: A venture-backed education technology startup that bridged the gap between student data and applications. I worked as an operations analyst & Python engineer.
  • Pearson: I worked at this education behemoth as a product manager on the mobile team.
  • Udemy: A SF-based online course platform for courses aimed at professional skills. I worked on the sales team.

    • I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2013, where I majored in Statistics with coursework in philosophy (logic, foundations of pure mathematics) and computer science.
